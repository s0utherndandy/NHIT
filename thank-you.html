About New Horizons

New Horizons Computer Learning Centers Inc. is the world's largest independent IT training company with 300 centers in 70 countries. At New Horizons, we focus on continued worldwide expansion to meet the ever-increasing demands of the technology evolution.

Innovative, award-winning learning methods such as Online LIVE(r) and Mentored Learning(r) have revolutionized the way students learn, retain and apply new knowledge.

New Horizons offers an extensive selection of vendor-authorized training and certifications for top technology providers such as Microsoft, Cisco and CompTIA.

Furthermore, we are the world's largest training provider for Microsoft, Cisco and CompTIA. Being an authorized partner ensures that our students have the highest quality training materials and train on the latest products and technologies available.

New Horizons has quickly become the world leader in this industry through its development of company-owned and franchised training centers.

Career College Group is excited to be the latest franchise here in Toronto and bring IT training to even more students!

Admissions Requirements

New Horizons has a four-step admissions process which includes contacting the campus, a confidential interview, a business affairs assessment and the final enrollment. In addition, New Horizons has three requirements that all future students must complete before enrolling into a diploma program.

  • Gr.12 OSSD/High School Graduate or Mature Student Status (18 Ontario, 19 out of province)
  • Successful completion of entrance evaluation
  • Admissions interview

Career Services

Employment counselling and job search training is provided for all students during their program by scheduled classroom lectures and ongoing individualized counselling. The School remains a permanent source of employment search assistance for all graduates. The placement department prepares each student to:

  • Create cover letter, resume and other effective portfolio material.
  • Conduct an employment interview including proper follow-up activities.
  • Analyze employment opportunities and market trends.
  • Utilize resources for locating prospective employers.
  • Analyze employment benefits and career advancement opportunities.

New Horizons maintains a list of current and past graduates indicating their initial place of employment, along with completion and placement percentages. A list of past and current employers is available to all prospective students.
